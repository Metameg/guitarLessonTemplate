The Instructor

The guitar is not just an instrument — it's a language. My goal is to help every student speak it fluently, authentically, and with their own voice.

Gary Coleman has dedicated over fifteen years to the craft of teaching guitar. With a formal education from the Berklee College of Music and a decade on the road as a touring guitarist, Gary brings both technical rigor and real-world musicianship to every lesson.

His teaching philosophy centers on building a strong foundation while nurturing the individual style of each student. Whether you're picking up your first guitar or looking to break through a plateau, Gary tailors every session to where you are and where you want to go.

Gary has instructed students across a diverse spectrum — from children learning their first chords to professional musicians seeking to expand their toolkit. His methodical yet creative approach has earned him a reputation as one of the most effective guitar instructors in the region.

What I Teach

Lesson Programs

Each program is tailored to your experience level, goals, and musical interests. All lessons are one-on-one and conducted either in-person or virtually.

01

Beginner Foundations

Start your journey the right way. Learn proper technique, chord shapes, and basic music theory from day one — so you never develop bad habits.

  • Open chords & strumming patterns
  • Reading tablature & chord charts
  • Basic music theory fundamentals
Inquire →
02

Intermediate Development

Break through plateaus. Expand your chord vocabulary, refine your picking technique, and start understanding song structure and arrangement.

  • Barre chords & power chords
  • Pentatonic & major scale shapes
  • Lead guitar fundamentals
Inquire →
03

Advanced Technique

For serious players. Deep dive into advanced concepts — sweep picking, tapping, hybrid picking, modal theory, and genre-specific mastery.

  • Advanced soloing & improvisation
  • Complex chord voicings & inversions
  • Modal theory & applications
Inquire →
04

Music Theory

Understand the language behind the music. Theory lessons accelerate growth across all instruments and genres, and unlock your creative potential.

  • Scales, modes & key signatures
  • Harmony, rhythm & notation
  • Ear training & transcription
Inquire →
05

Classical Guitar

Develop exquisite fingerstyle technique through the classical tradition. Study composers from Sor to Villa-Lobos while mastering nail care, posture, and expression.

  • Classical posture & right-hand technique
  • Sight-reading standard notation
  • Repertoire: Baroque to Modern
Inquire →
06

Songwriting

Turn ideas into complete songs. Learn to craft chord progressions, melodies, lyrics, and arrangements that connect with listeners and reflect your artistic voice.

  • Chord progressions & song structure
  • Melody writing & lyric development
  • Recording basics & demos
